About

I’m Michelle— a product manager. I care a lot about using technology for positive impact. Currently based in Toronto, Canada.

I am currently studying Systems Design Engineering at the University of Waterloo. I am a systems thinker with an analytical and creative mind. I am incredibly passionate about problem solving and creating innovative designs to enhance our everyday lives.

My motivation lies in my desire to develop my own impactful solutions to real-world problems. I am eager to answer difficult technical problems that require innovation and invention. I am deeply excited about technology and its potential to bring about meaningful change. My goal is to contribute to this change.

In my free time you will find me discovering new music , exploring different cultures through travel , and enjoying various sports .
